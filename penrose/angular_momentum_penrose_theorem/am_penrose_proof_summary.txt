CONJECTURE:
-----------
For asymptotically flat, axisymmetric initial data (M³, g, K) satisfying DEC
with outermost MOTS Σ of area A and Komar angular momentum J:

    M_ADM ≥ √(A/(16π) + 4πJ²/A)

Equality holds for Kerr spacetime.

KEY MODIFICATIONS TO SPACETIME PENROSE PROOF:
=============================================

1. JANG EQUATION WITH TWIST
   Standard: H_Γ(f) = tr_Γ(f) K
   Modified: H_Γ(f) - tr_Γ(f) K^{sym} = S_ω[f]
   
   The twist source S_ω[f] captures rotational effects from K^{twist}.
   Key property: Cylindrical blowup at MOTS is preserved.

2. CONFORMAL SEALING WITH J
   Standard: -8Δφ + R_bar φ = 0
   Modified: -8Δφ + R_bar φ + Λ_J φ^{-7} = 0
   
   The φ^{-7} term is the standard York conformal contribution from
   the transverse-traceless part of K (angular momentum content).

3. AMO FUNCTIONAL MODIFICATION
   Standard: M_p(t) → √(A(t)/(16π)) as p → 1
   Modified: M_{p,J}(t) → √(A(t)/(16π) + 4πJ²/A(t))
   
   The J²/A term vanishes at infinity, giving M_ADM as outer boundary value.

4. BOUNDARY VALUES
   At horizon (t=0): M_{1,J}(0) = √(A/(16π) + 4πJ²/A) [TARGET]
   At infinity (t→1): M_{1,J}(1) = M_ADM [KNOWN]
   
   Monotonicity yields AM-Penrose inequality.
